How It All Started

Born From a Booster Pack

Kip grew up right here in Los Altos, spending his allowance on Base Set packs from the corner store. By the time he was in high school, his binder of holos was the stuff of local legend.

After a decade of collecting professionally — attending card shows from Vegas to Chicago, building relationships with graders and auction houses — Kip opened the shop in 2009 with a simple mission: give collectors in the South Bay a place they actually deserve.

That means curated inventory without the filler. Honest buylist prices. A team that treats every slab like it's their own. And a shop that looks as good as the cards inside it.

What We Stand For

Our Pillars

🏆
Authenticity First

Every graded slab is verified. Every autograph authenticated. We don't cut corners on provenance — because your trust is worth more than a quick sale.

🔬
Expert Knowledge

Our team has decades of combined experience. We can tell you which PSA 10 populations are tightening, which sets are undervalued, and when to hold vs. sell.

🤝
Community Roots

We host weekly tournaments, draft nights, and trading events. This isn't just a shop — it's a hub for the entire Bay Area collecting community.

💎
Curated Inventory

We don't just stock everything — we stock the right things. If it's on our shelf, we believe in it. No filler packs, no overpriced bulk.

📦
Fair Buylist

Selling your collection? We'll give you a real offer based on market data — not the lowest number we can get away with. We want your business long-term.

🚀
Always Evolving

The hobby moves fast. We stay ahead of new releases, grading trends, and market shifts so you never have to wonder if you're getting current information.
