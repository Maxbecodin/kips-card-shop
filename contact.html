Common Questions

FAQ

Do you buy individual cards or only full collections? +

Both. We buy everything from single high-value singles ($50+) to complete collections. Bring your cards in-store or send us photos via Instagram DM for a quick quote.

How long does grading take through your submission service? +

PSA and BGS turnaround varies by service level — typically 60–120 days for standard, 20–45 days for express. We batch submissions to minimize per-card fees. Ask us in store about current submission cycles.

Do you hold cards or offer layaway? +

Yes — we offer layaway on items $200 and above. A 25% deposit holds the item for up to 30 days. Ask in store for details.

Do you ship? +

Sealed product ships anywhere in the US. Graded slabs and high-value singles are handled on a case-by-case basis with full insurance. Contact us before placing an order.
