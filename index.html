What We Carry

Whether you're completing a vintage set, hunting today's hottest rookies, or just want to rip some packs — Kip's has you covered.

⚡
Pokémon TCG

Singles, booster packs, elite trainer boxes, and sealed products spanning vintage Base Set all the way to today's newest releases.

🏆
Sports Cards

Baseball, basketball, football, and hockey — vintage stars, modern rookies, refractors, and autographs across all major brands.

🔖
Graded Slabs

PSA, BGS, and CGC graded cards for the serious collector. We also help you submit your cards for grading — we do all the work.

⭐
Memorabilia

Signed items, game-used pieces, framed displays, and authenticated sports collectibles from your favorite athletes.

📦
Packs & Boxes

Deep inventory of sealed packs and boxes — vintage and modern, Pokémon and sports. Always rotating, always fresh.

🤝
Buy & Trade

We buy and trade collections. Bring in your cards — if the offer is right, we're interested. Always fair, always honest.

About the Owner

A Childhood Dream
Turned Reality

Cisco, a Los Altos Hills resident, turned his lifelong passion for collecting into Kip's Card Shop — the Bay Area's most welcoming spot for card collectors of all levels.

Featured in the Los Altos Online, Cisco built Kip's on a simple idea: a shop where collectors feel at home, get fair deals, and always find something special.

Expert grading consultation — PSA, JSA, and BGS submissions handled for you
Helping investors build and grade valuable collections
Deep inventory of vintage and modern cards — always fresh stock
Happy to track down specific cards or sets — just ask!
